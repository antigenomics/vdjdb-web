Batch annotation of TCR sequences

Connecting

Here you can upload your T-cell receptor repertoire sequencing data and annotate it by performing batch queries of the VDJdb database.

To proceed with the analysis, go to the upload section, add your samples, and click on the sample name in the panel on the left. After running the annotation, you can view the table of VDJdb matches (click Table below the sample name) and annotation summary charts (click Charts below the sample name).

If you want to annotate all your samples together and get a summary statistics report for your dataset, click All samples in the panel on the left.


Note that limits on the number of samples, sample file size and number of clonotypes (unique TCR sequences) apply. Please see this section of VDJtools documentation for the list of supported input formats.
